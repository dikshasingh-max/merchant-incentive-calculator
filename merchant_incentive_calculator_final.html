How it works
  1. 1Click Open Looker. Filter by Merchant ID or Merchant Name. The query returns the last 6 months in the merchant's local currency.
  2. 2Click Run, then Download → Excel (.xlsx).
  3. 3Drop the file below. Merchant name, venue count, GOV, subtotal, commission rate, courier costs and all P&L lines auto-populate from the L6M average.
  4. 4Choose scope (venue or merchant level), configure incentive tiers, and review the VP output.
  5. 5Screenshot and submit via the Slack workflow in #merchant-incentive-requests.

Eligibility & rules
  • Enterprise, Mid-Market or Tier 1 · On platform 8–12+ months · VP positive
  • Base CR above country/tier average · No self-delivery only merchants
  • One target metric per merchant (GOV or Subtotal) · One incentive type (% rebate or fixed fee)
  • Rebate cap: max 3% of GOV per period

How to submit
